No Bake Cheesecake

Recipe From No-Bake Lemon Pie AllRecipes.com

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, room temperature
  • 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • ⅓ cup lemon juice
  • 1 (9 inch) prepared graham cracker crust
  • ½ cup whipped cream for serving

Directions

Mix together the cream cheese, milk and lemon juice. Mix well and spread in graham cracker crust. Chill until set, at least 2 hours, and top as desired.
